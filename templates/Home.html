Core Features

AI Disease Prediction

Enter your symptoms and our AI will conduct a guided conversation to predict possible illnesses and suggest safe, generic over-the-counter remedies.

Prescription Analysis

Upload a prescription PDF. Our RAG-supported AI analyzes it for dosage safety, potential allergic reactions, and drug interactions based on your profile.

Downloadable Reports

Every diagnosis session generates a comprehensive, downloadable PDF report with your symptoms, AI analysis, possible conditions, and recommended next steps.

Why SehatSathi AI Matters

The Problem
Lack of Access

In many parts of India, immediate access to medical advice is a challenge, leading to delayed care and self-medication risks.

The Solution
AI as a First Doctor

SehatSathi AI acts as a virtual doctor, offering instant guidance for non-emergency situations and flagging potential risks in prescriptions.

Our Mission
Empowerment

To provide a safe, reliable, and accessible first point of contact for healthcare queries, empowering users to make informed decisions.

The Impact
Safer Healthcare

By promoting awareness and providing data-driven suggestions, we aim to reduce medication errors and encourage timely professional consultation.

About SehatSathi AI

About SehatSathi AI

SehatSathi AI ("Health Companion AI") is an innovative project designed to bridge the gap in primary healthcare access. Leveraging the power of large language models and retrieval-augmented generation, we offer a comprehensive safety net for initial medical queries.

Our goal is to make healthcare guidance more accessible and safer for everyone, from bustling cities to remote villages.
